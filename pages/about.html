Our Story

Who We Are

Anniyappa Publications, founded under SB Institute, has been a trusted name in educational publishing since 2009. We specialize in creating high-quality academic books aligned with Tamil Nadu university syllabi.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible to every student through affordable, well-structured, and exam-focused study materials.

Our Mission
